After a dog bite or animal attack

  • Collect the owner’s and witnesses’ information

Liability & compensation

Florida law generally holds dog owners liable when their dog bites a person who is lawfully on public or private property. We pursue recovery for medical bills, lost wages, pain and suffering, and emotional distress.

How we help

  • Investigate ownership and coverage
  • Preserve evidence and document injuries
  • Handle insurers and negotiations
  • File suit when necessary
